AfroPay gives your business a single REST API to move money across Africa's most popular mobile money networks — including M-PESA and Airtel Money. Whether you need to collect payments from customers (C2B) or pay out funds to their wallets (B2C), AfroPay handles the network complexity so you can focus on building your product.

## How to get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get your credentials">
    Sign in to the [AfroPay dashboard](https://dashboard.afropay.io) and retrieve your `client_id` and `client_secret` from the **API Keys** section of your account settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authenticate">
    Exchange your credentials for a short-lived OAuth2 bearer token by calling `POST /oauth/token`. Include this token in the `Authorization` header of every subsequent request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make your first API call">
    Initiate a collection by calling `POST /api/v1/c2b` with the customer's phone number, amount, and currency. AfroPay routes the request to the correct mobile money network automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Handle the result">
    Receive the final payment outcome in real time via a webhook event sent to your registered callback URL, or poll `GET /api/v1/transaction/{referenceId}` for the current status.
  </Step>
</Steps>
